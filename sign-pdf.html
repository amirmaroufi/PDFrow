How to Sign PDF Documents

Simple 3-step process to add signatures to your PDF files

📁 1

Upload Your PDF

Select the PDF document you want to sign. Your file is processed locally in your browser for maximum security.

• Supports up to 50MB files • Multi-page documents • Secure local processing
✍️ 2

Create Your Signature

Choose from multiple signature options: draw with your mouse, upload an image, or create a typed signature with custom fonts.

• Draw: Natural signature with mouse or touch • Upload: Use existing signature images • Type: Professional text signatures
📍 3

Position & Download

Drag your signature to the desired location, resize as needed, and download your signed PDF document.

• Drag and drop positioning • Resizable signatures • Instant download
