    How to Organize PDF Pages

    Simple 3-step process to organize your PDF pages with drag and drop

    📁 1

    Upload Your PDF

    Select your PDF file from your device, or simply drag and drop it into the upload area. The file is processed locally for maximum security.

    • Supports up to 50MB per file • Instant page preview • Secure local processing
    📑 2

    Organize Pages

    Use drag and drop to reorder pages, delete unwanted pages, or select multiple pages for bulk operations. See real-time previews of your changes.

    • Drag & drop: Reorder pages easily • Delete pages: Remove unwanted content • Multi-select: Bulk operations support
    ⬇️ 3

    Download & Save

    Download your newly organized PDF with all changes applied. The file maintains its original quality with your custom page arrangement.

    • Instant download available • Original quality maintained • Custom file naming
